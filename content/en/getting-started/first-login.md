## Before You Start

- You need an invitation from your workspace admin or the Flete24 team.
- You will receive a magic link by email to complete your first login — no password required.

---

## Steps

### Step 1 — Open the magic link email

Check your inbox for an email from `noreply@flete24.com` with subject **"Your Flete24 sign-in link"**. Click the link.

> **Tip:** If you don't see it, check your spam folder. The link expires in 24 hours.

### Step 2 — Complete sign-in

The link opens the Flete24 app and signs you in automatically. You will land on the Dashboard.

### Step 3 — Check your workspace

In the top-left corner, you will see your tenant name (e.g. "Global 506 Logistics"). This is your active workspace.

If you belong to more than one tenant, click the tenant name to open the switcher and select the correct workspace.

### Step 4 — Set up your profile

Go to **Settings → Profile** to update your name and preferences.

---

## What Happens Next

Once logged in, you have access to the full agent workspace: Directory, Rates, Quotes, Local Charges, and Connections.

---

## Common Issues

| Problem               | Solution                                                 |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Magic link expired    | Request a new link from the login page                   |
| Wrong workspace shown | Use the tenant switcher (top-left) to change workspaces  |
| No email received     | Check spam, confirm your email with your workspace admin |

## Overview

Flete24 has three user roles. Your role determines which features you can access and what actions you can take.

---

## Agent (Freight Forwarder / Carrier)

Agents are logistics service providers — freight forwarders, 3PLs, 4PLs, and carriers.

**What agents can do:**

- Manage a rate library (ocean freight, local charges)
- Import rates from Excel or extract them from documents using AI
- Build and send quotes to BCO clients
- Manage a directory of contacts and companies
- Connect with BCO partners on the platform

**Typical companies:** Global 506 Logistics, Flex Cargo International

---

## BCO (Beneficial Cargo Owner)

BCOs are cargo owners — importers, exporters, and manufacturers who ship goods.

**What BCOs can do _(coming soon)_:**

- Receive quotes from connected agents
- Compare rates across multiple forwarders
- Track active shipments and milestones
- Manage documents per booking

---

## Platform Admin

Platform admins manage the Flete24 platform itself. This role is internal to the Flete24 team.

**What admins can do:**

- Manage all tenants and users
- View platform-wide AI usage and costs
- Manage reference data (carriers, container types, locations)

---

## Tenant vs User

A **tenant** is a company (e.g. "Global 506 Logistics"). A **user** is a person who belongs to one or more tenants. One user can be a member of multiple tenants and switch between them using the tenant switcher in the top navigation.
